The Museum

On April 27, 2017, the Jackie Robinson Foundation held a groundbreaking ceremony for the Jackie Robinson Museum, at 75 Varick Street, in lower Manhattan, New York City, US. The 18,500 square-foot space acts as a permanent tribute to Jackie Robinson's pioneering legacy and role as a catalyst for the Civil Rights Movement. The museum will serve as a venue for dialogue on critical social issues and as a destination for innovative educational programming. Read More
